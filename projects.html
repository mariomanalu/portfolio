Welcome to my project homepage!



This page contains some personal projects that I have finished for my own fulfillment. I name my projects after major cities that I have visited in reversed chronological order, so the first city you see is the my most recent destination. I decided to use this naming system to remind myself of places I have been and how big the world is, plus when I run out of names for my future projects, which indicates that I have not been travelling for a while, I will be encouraged to visit a new place.



Feel free to scroll around. The images are "href"-ed. You can click them, and they will direct you to the corresponding page. There is no "Back" button, instead you could click on the logo at the top left corner, and it will direct you to the home page.
